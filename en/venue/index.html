COSCUP 2011 Meeting Place

Location

  • International Conference Hall, Humanities & Social Sciences Building, Academia Sinica, Taipei, Taiwan (No.128, Sec. 2, Academia Rd., Nangang Dist., Taipei City 115, Taiwan)

Transportation

  • Location and Map of Academia Sinica
  • Academia Sinica Campus: No.24, Humanities and Social Sciences Building (HSSB)

Transportation to Academia Sinica

  • Take Bus 212, 276, 306, 620 at Metro Nangang Exhibition Center (Map)
  • Get off at Academia Sinica Stop

Transportation from Taoyuan Int’l Airport to downtown Taipei

Shuttle bus to Downtown

  • Service Time: 6:00 ~ 24:00
  • Fare: NT$110 ~ NT$140 per adult
  • Journey Time: 60 mins ~ 90 mins

High speed trains via shuttle bus to station

  • Service Time: 7:00 ~ 22:00
  • Fare: NT$30 (shuttle bus), NT$140 (high speed rail)
  • Journey Time: about 1 hour

Taxi

  • Service Time: 24 hours a day
  • Fare: about NT$1200 (based on the meter plus a 15% surcharge and highway tolls)
  • Journey Time: about 1 hour
